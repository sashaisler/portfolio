Mini Musical Chairs

Tools & Technologies

C++, Arduino, Fusion 360, 3D printing

Description

Mini Musical Chairs is a game I developed with a team of three other students as part of a class assignment. We were tasked with building a game using an Arduino over the course of a semester to be showcased at an end-of-semester expo. I led my team in creating our project: a finger-operated adaptation of musical chairs, designed for engaging gameplay through simple hand movements.

Mini Musical Chairs allows for up to 6 players, uses both a speaker and an LED strip to signal the end of a round, and each round lasts for a random amount of time to add the classic element of suspense to the game.
